# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors and maintainers commit to fostering an open, welcoming environment. Participation will be harassment-free for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes
- Prioritizing community welfare

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- Sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing private information without permission
- Conduct inappropriate in professional settings

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers clarify acceptable standards and take fair corrective action against inappropriate behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and contributions misaligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code applies within community spaces and when officially representing the community publicly (official email, social media, appointed events).

## Enforcement

Report violations to antoni@golf.dev. All complaints receive prompt, fair review. Leaders respect reporter privacy and security.

## Enforcement Guidelines

**1. Correction** - Inappropriate language in single incidents receives private written warning with clarity on violations.

**2. Warning** - Sustained violations result in warnings prohibiting interaction for specified periods; violation may lead to permanent ban.

**3. Temporary Ban** - Serious sustained violations incur temporary public interaction bans; subsequent violations may trigger permanent ban.

**4. Permanent Ban** - Pattern violations demonstrating harassment or aggression result in permanent community exclusion.
